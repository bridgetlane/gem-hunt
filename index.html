Gem Hunt

  • High Concept: Play as an reluctant participant collecting gems. He has to be persuaded, because he is a reluctant participant.

  • Player Experience Goals: The player experience should be unpredictable because of the lava, and cause the player to take note of what they are doing.

  • Genre: Casual games.

  • Game play: Players can use L/R/U/D arrow keys to navigate the maze and collect all eight gems on the map.

    If you hit lava, the lava will expand on the map and your player will become more disgruntled.

    If you hit water, your player will drink the water and you will gain persuasion.
    System dynamics: The lava expands on hit.
    Core mechanic: You can collect gems.

  • Prototype goal: This prototype explores careful controls.

  • Features: My game is unique because if you hit the lava you will most likely fail.

  • Setting/Premise: You are trying to collect all the gems, but you are playing as a reluctant participant (he does not want to help you). Therefore he easily becomes disgruntled.

  • Story: The game does not have an storyline.

  • Target Audience: Casual gamers.

  • Play time: 3 minutes.

  • Strategies: Keep an eye on persuasion.
